Research Interests

I am interested in Smart Meter Data Analytics, Data Management, Big Data, and Data Mining. My current research includes:

  • Smart meter data analytics
  • Energy Informatics
  • Big data management, and ETL
  • Internet of Things

Smart Meter Data Analytics

My research in smart meter data analytics focuses on:

  • Benchmarking smart meter data analytics systems and algorithms
  • Developing smart meter data analytics systems
  • Detecting anomalous energy consumption patterns
  • Applying machine learning techniques to energy consumption data

This research aims to help utilities and consumers better understand energy usage patterns and identify opportunities for energy conservation.

Energy Informatics

My work in energy informatics explores the intersection of information systems and energy systems, with a focus on:

  • Energy data management and analytics
  • Smart grid technologies
  • Renewable energy integration
  • Energy efficiency optimization

Big Data Management

In the area of big data management, my research includes:

  • Scalable data processing frameworks
  • ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes for big data
  • Data warehousing for time series data
  • Performance optimization for big data systems

Internet of Things

My IoT research focuses on:

  • IoT data collection and processing
  • Edge computing for IoT applications
  • IoT analytics for smart buildings and cities
  • Integration of IoT with energy systems
